How It Works

Positive Sentiment

Text with a polarity score greater than 0.1 is classified as positive. This includes words like "good", "excellent", "love", etc.

Negative Sentiment

Text with a polarity score less than -0.1 is classified as negative. This includes words like "bad", "terrible", "hate", etc.

Neutral Sentiment

Text with a polarity score between -0.1 and 0.1 is classified as neutral. This includes factual statements without emotional language.
